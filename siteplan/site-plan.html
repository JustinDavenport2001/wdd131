Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my site is to help people find reliable, high-quality camping spots and enjoyable hiking trails. I want to make it easier for outdoor lovers to discover new places, learn what to expect, and plan their adventures with confidence.

Audience

My audience is hikers and campers between the ages of 20–65 who enjoy exploring the outdoors. They value clear information, good recommendations, and simple tools that help them choose the best trails and campsites for their experience level.

Dynamic elements

My site will use JavaScript to make the experience more interactive and helpful for users. One dynamic element will be a comment form, where visitors can write reviews or notes about camping spots or hiking trails. When the form is submitted, JavaScript will validate the input, save the comment to an array or localStorage, and display it instantly on the page without reloading. Another dynamic feature will be a menu of places to go (camping spots or hikes) that can grow over time. Users will be able to add new locations to the list using a small form. JavaScript will store each new place in an object, push it into an array, and render it on the page using DOM manipulation. This will also include sorting or filtering options so users can quickly find a spot that fits their needs. My JavaScript will use modules, multiple functions, conditional checks, event listeners, and array methods like forEach, map, and filter to organize and display the data in an efficient and user-friendly way.

Content

Home page

Home Page Content Welcome to Trail & Camp Finder Finding the perfect spot for your next adventure shouldn’t be stressful. This site helps you discover reliable camping areas and great hiking trails, all recommended by people who love the outdoors just as much as you do. Whether you’re planning a weekend getaway or a long hike through mountain backcountry, this site gives you the information you need to get out there with confidence. Explore New Places Browse through a growing list of camping spots and hikes. Each location includes quick details like difficulty, distance, scenery, and tips from other hikers and campers. You can sort or filter spots based on what you're looking for—easy trails, quiet campsites, scenic views, or beginner-friendly areas. Share Your Experience Your feedback helps other adventurers. Use the comment form to leave a review or share something useful you noticed on the trail or at the campsite. Good advice goes a long way in the outdoors.

[Page 2]

Child Page: Places to Explore Find Your Next Outdoor Destination This page lists all the current trails and campsites. Each entry includes basic information like location, terrain, difficulty, and recommended gear. The list will keep growing as users submit new places. Add a New Place If you know a hidden gem or a spot worth visiting, you can add it to the menu using the form on this page. Just fill in the name, type of location (hike or campsite), and a brief description. Once submitted, JavaScript will add it to the list automatically without reloading the page. Recent Comments See what other hikers and campers are saying. Comments include personal tips, things to watch out for, and what people enjoyed most about the place. You can leave your own comment to help make the outdoors easier and safer for others.
